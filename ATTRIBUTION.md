# Attribution Requirements

Attribution is required when you copy, build, modify, publish, distribute, demonstrate, or document CryoSnap-derived work.

## Standard attribution

Use this attribution for unmodified use:

```text
Based on CryoSnap by Sheetak, Inc.
Used under the CryoSnap community license terms.
```

Include a link to the original repository where practical:

```text
Based on CryoSnap by Sheetak, Inc.
Original repository: [repository URL]
Used under the CryoSnap community license terms.
```

## Attribution for modified versions

Use this attribution for modified or derivative work:

```text
Derived from CryoSnap by Sheetak, Inc.
Modified by [your name or organization].
Sheetak does not endorse or certify this derivative work.
```

## Where attribution should appear

Attribution should appear in at least one reasonable location associated with the work, such as:

- the derivative repository README
- documentation
- a product page
- an academic paper or project report
- a demo page
- a bill of materials or design notes file
- firmware about/help output, where practical
- packaging or printed material, where practical

For PCB derivatives, attribution should be preserved in design metadata and documentation. PCB silkscreen attribution is encouraged where practical, but is not required when space, cost, manufacturability, or electrical constraints make it unreasonable.

## Notice preservation

Do not remove or obscure existing Sheetak copyright notices, license notices, required notices, or attribution statements from source files, schematic files, PCB files, documentation, or metadata.

## No endorsement

Attribution must not imply that Sheetak endorses, certifies, sponsors, supports, or approves a derivative work unless Sheetak has separately agreed in writing.

Do not use wording such as:

- Sheetak approved
- Sheetak certified
- official CryoSnap
- official Sheetak product
- manufactured by Sheetak
- supported by Sheetak

unless Sheetak has authorized that wording in writing.

## Short-form references

Acceptable short-form references include:

- "Based on CryoSnap by Sheetak"
- "Derived from CryoSnap by Sheetak"
- "Uses CryoSnap reference design files from Sheetak"

These references are allowed only as attribution or nominative description. They do not grant trademark rights or commercial authorization.
