# Commercial Use of CryoSnap

CryoSnap is made public to encourage adoption, evaluation, education, experimentation, internal use, and community development.

Commercial use requires prior written authorization from Sheetak.

## Commercial use requires authorization

Contact Sheetak before doing any of the following with CryoSnap or CryoSnap-derived work:

- selling PCBs, assemblies, kits, modules, devices, or products based on CryoSnap
- selling modified versions of CryoSnap hardware or firmware
- distributing CryoSnap-derived firmware, binaries, boards, assemblies, or design files as part of a paid product
- integrating CryoSnap-derived hardware or firmware into a product sold or licensed to customers
- using CryoSnap-derived work in a paid customer deliverable
- offering paid consulting, integration, testing, training, support, or validation services based primarily on CryoSnap
- contract manufacturing CryoSnap-derived boards or assemblies for third parties
- marketing a product as CryoSnap, Sheetak, Sheetak-approved, Sheetak-certified, or CryoSnap-compatible without written authorization
- using Sheetak or CryoSnap trademarks beyond the limited nominative uses allowed in [TRADEMARKS.md](TRADEMARKS.md)

## Uses that generally do not require commercial authorization

Subject to [LICENSE.md](LICENSE.md), [ATTRIBUTION.md](ATTRIBUTION.md), [NOTICE.md](NOTICE.md), and [TRADEMARKS.md](TRADEMARKS.md), the following generally do not require separate commercial authorization:

- personal projects
- hobby builds
- student projects
- classroom use
- public research
- academic research
- internal evaluation
- internal engineering prototypes
- internal lab fixtures
- internal thermal characterization
- internal firmware testing
- internal design exploration
- non-commercial publication of modifications with attribution

These examples are not a waiver of the license terms. If your use is tied to selling, licensing, distributing, or delivering a product or service to customers, ask first.

## Authorization may be free

Sheetak may grant commercial authorization at no cost in some cases, including evaluation partnerships, pilot projects, ecosystem collaborations, educational distribution, research collaborations, or other uses that support the CryoSnap community.

Sheetak may also offer paid commercial licenses, production licenses, support agreements, certification programs, or partnership agreements.

## How to request authorization

Send a request to:

```text
info@sheetak.com
```

Please include:

- your name and organization
- contact information
- intended product, service, or distribution model
- whether you are using CryoSnap as-is or with modifications
- which parts are used: firmware, PCB design, BOM, documentation, examples, or all of the above
- expected volumes, if physical products are involved
- whether you need Sheetak support, validation, certification, or supply-chain assistance
- target launch date or pilot date
- whether Sheetak or CryoSnap branding would appear in marketing, packaging, documentation, or product pages

## No implied authorization

A public GitHub repository, issue response, pull request review, discussion comment, or technical answer from Sheetak does not grant commercial authorization.

Commercial authorization must be in a written agreement signed or otherwise expressly approved by Sheetak.

## No endorsement

Unless Sheetak separately agrees in writing, Sheetak does not endorse, certify, warrant, support, or approve any derivative work.
