# Trademark Guidelines

This repository does not grant any ownership interest or license in Sheetak trademarks, service marks, logos, trade names, product names, or trade dress.

## Sheetak marks

Sheetak marks include, without limitation:

- Sheetak
- CryoSnap
- Sheetak logos
- CryoSnap logos
- other Sheetak product names, marks, graphics, badges, or branding

## Permitted nominative use

You may use the names "Sheetak" and "CryoSnap" solely to describe the origin of the project or to provide required attribution, such as:

```text
Based on CryoSnap by Sheetak, Inc.
```

or:

```text
Derived from CryoSnap by Sheetak, Inc.
```

## Not permitted without written authorization

You may not, without prior written authorization from Sheetak:

- use Sheetak logos
- use CryoSnap logos
- name your product "CryoSnap"
- use a confusingly similar name
- imply Sheetak sponsorship, endorsement, certification, approval, support, partnership, or affiliation
- market a derivative product as an official Sheetak product
- market a derivative product as an official CryoSnap product
- use "Sheetak-certified", "Sheetak-approved", "official CryoSnap", or similar claims
- use Sheetak marks in advertising, product packaging, storefront listings, or paid services except as required attribution or nominative description

## Compatibility claims

Do not claim that a product is "CryoSnap-compatible", "CryoSnap-certified", "Sheetak-compatible", or "Sheetak-certified" unless Sheetak has authorized that claim in writing.

You may use factual, non-confusing statements such as:

```text
This project was derived from the CryoSnap reference design by Sheetak, Inc.
```

or:

```text
This project was tested with an unmodified CryoSnap board.
```

provided the statement is true, not misleading, and accompanied by the no-endorsement statement below.

## Required no-endorsement statement for derivatives

Derivative works should include:

```text
Sheetak does not endorse, certify, warrant, or support this derivative work.
```

## Commercial branding

Commercial use of Sheetak or CryoSnap branding requires a separate written agreement with Sheetak. See [COMMERCIAL.md](COMMERCIAL.md).
